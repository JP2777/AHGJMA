Classes

Little Lions

Program for 6-7 yearc olds.
Designed to develop basic motor skills, balance, and hand-eye coordination. The class is a perfect blend of focus and fitness set in a fun and nurturing environment.

Juniors Class

High energy streamlined martial arts program designed by American Heritage Goju Karate-Do!

Students in this class are taught a variety of self defense/fighting techniques in a moving, structured and friendly environment.

Adult Class

Martial arts program designed for adults and teens ages 12 and up. Students will be taught a variety of self defense/fighting techniques. Students develop a stronger/healthier mind, body and spirit through this training. Program includes meditation and relaxation techniques for stress management.
